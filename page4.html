recommendations for contamination in food processing

If contaminated water or ice is used to wash, pack, or chill fruit or vegetables, the contamination can spread to those items and we recommend to filter the factory water before using it to wash the food.

During the slaughter process, germs on an animalâ€™s hide that came from the intestines can get into the final meat product. After the slaughter process, put the animal in a sterilization device for a short.

If germs contaminate surfaces used for food processing, such as a processing line or storage bins, germs can spread to foods that touch those surfaces. As a solution, disinfection of storage boxes and food packaging after the processing line can be used.


recommendations for contamination in food distribution

Drivers should be supplied with an alcohol-based hand sanitizer, a disinfectant, and paper towels. Drivers should use a hand sanitizer before passing delivery documents to food premises staff. Disposable containers and packaging should be used to avoid the need for cleaning of any returns. In the case of reusable containers, appropriate hygiene and sanitation protocols should be implemented.
